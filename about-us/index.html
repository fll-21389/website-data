About Our Team


We're First Lego League team 21389! Our team name is "The Pyrates." This is a play on Python, which is the language we are using to program out robot this year. We are located in Westfield, Indiana and have 3 teammates. Our teammates are in 5th, 6th, and 8th grade. All three of our teammates have multiple years of experience, and one of our teamates has qualifed for the World competition. This year we are aiming for Worlds again and think we will get there based on our experience, robot design, and how well our team operates. If you have any questions please reach out to us on our contact page or via email, which will appear here in the near future. This entire website is hand-written from scratch by Alex.
