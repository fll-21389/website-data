The Products We Design

At Safe Steps, we design and build products with 4 main goals: To make safe, quality products that are easy to use and benefit the public. At this time, we have 10 different products that we sell online.

The Company

We have 3 administrative employees at the Safe Steps company. Nick Del Duco is our Chief Executive Officer, Alex McMinn is the Chief Financial Officer, and Michael Quirke is the Chief Marketing Officer.

In addition to our administrative employees, we have several factory employees and product designers.
