The Products We Design

At UniSystems, we design and build products with 4 main goals: To make safe, quality products that are easy to use and benefit the public. At this time, we have 3 different products that we sell online.


Our products have a profound difference from the current leading fountain dispensers. Our products automatically dispense the customer's drink of choice based on either what they ordered that day or what they have set previously through our app. Our products are designed so that you can get the same drinks at every participating store location, whether the product is a Coca-Cola or Pepsi product.

The Ordering Process

We have made an easy and streamlined process for ordering the right drink. Customers order the food exactly how they normally would, and then they order a drink along with it. They are then handed a cup to go get scanned at the drink machine. They simply place the cup into the dispenser and it will automatically fill the right drink to the right amount. The store can also use this method to automatically set limits on how many refills people can get.


To automatically order from a preset, simply have the cashier scan the preset barcode from the our official app, available on iOS and Android. From there, the process is the same. These presets can be used at any UniDispense across the world.
